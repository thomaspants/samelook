About Samelook

We've spent too much time looking for pants that work. Too many pairs of ill-fitting pants spend years in the closet or the bottom of the drawer. It's tedious and expensive visiting the tailor each time you buy, and cuts change fit from season to season and factory to factory. While we wait for body scans and true custom production to outfit the the world with perfect trousers there's a better way.

Samelook is about the confidence and convenience of a perfect fit everytime. But it's also about the sustainability of buying and owning less - bolstered by domestic production. Our products are your products. High quality materials and production worthy of top labels, with a fit that always works for you.



How Does It Work?

Have a pair of jeans you'd like as chinos? Perfect slacks you want in a new color? Our expert pattern makers will create a personalized pattern based on your favorite pants, then we produce a pair of pants with your pattern. Repeat as many times as you'd like once your pattern is on file.

When you make a purchase with Samelook we ship you a prepaid mailer. Use this mailer to send the pants you'd like copied to our pattern center. We'll draft your custom pattern, digitize it, and keep it on record for production. You'll receive your original pants back right away, followed by your new, Samelook pants shortly thereafter.

Currently it is required to mail in your pants. However, we are developing the methods necessary to product your custom pattern from a detailed set of measurements you can perform on your garments at home. Stay tuned!
